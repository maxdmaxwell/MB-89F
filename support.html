MB 89F — Support

Standard Fraction Calculator for the shop and everyday math

MB 89F is a standard calculator that works in exact fractions — every value is kept as a whole-number numerator over a whole-number denominator, so the math never drifts. You key a calculation the natural way and press equals. It has mixed-number entry, tape-measure rounding (1/8 to 1/64), fraction ↔ decimal conversion, unit tagging and conversion (IN / FT / MM / MT), and a built-in user manual and cheat sheet.

Getting help inside the app

Tap the gear icon (top-right) to open Settings, then choose User Manual for a full walkthrough or Cheat Sheet for a quick key reference. Most questions are answered there.

Frequently asked questions

How do I enter a fraction?

Use the b/c key: press 3 b/c 4 for 3/4. For a mixed number, use SPC for the gap: 2 SPC 1 b/c 2 gives 2 1/2.

How do I do a calculation?

Just like a normal calculator. Key a number, press an operator, key the next number, then press =. Example: 3 b/c 4 + 1 b/c 2 = shows 1 1/4. Operations chain left to right, and pressing = again repeats the last operation.

What do the gold Φ and blue Γ keys do?

They are shift keys. Φ reaches the gold labels (mode, decimal places, fraction/decimal conversion, store to memory, clear-all). Γ reaches the blue labels (1/x, x², ½x to find a centerline, 2x to double, and recall from memory). Square root has its own √x key.

How do I round to a tape-measure fraction?

Press 1/8, 1/16, 1/32, or 1/64 to round the displayed value to that increment. Press TRU to restore the exact original value.

How do I convert between inches and millimeters?

Tag a value by tapping IN, FT, MM, or MT. Then tap a different unit key to convert to it exactly.
